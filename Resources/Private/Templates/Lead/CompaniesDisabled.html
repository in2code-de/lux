GDPR conform Wiredminds integration into TYPO3

Just add a Wiredminds Token and convert your visitors to companies automatically :

Adding and submitting a valid token will add these TypoScript constants to your root templates :

  • plugin.tx_lux.settings.tracking.company.enable = 1
  • plugin.tx_lux.settings.tracking.company.token = [Wiredminds Token]
  • plugin.tx_lux.settings.tracking.company.connectionLimit = 5000
  • plugin.tx_lux.settings.tracking.company.autoConvert.enable = 1
